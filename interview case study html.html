conclusions¶

1) credit card is the most used method of payment by customers. 2) Telephony,auto,health_beauty,stationery ,bed_bath_table are the top five most expensive products. 3) Some of the most heavy products are home appliances , furnitures , bed bath table and housewares. 4) 'auto' is one of those few products which are both , expensive and also liked by customers since it is having a good average review score. 5) The bigger and heavy products are not necessarily , the most expensive ones.

recommendations for further studies¶

1)The data on the monthly or yearly sales of each of these items can be collected to further identify , which of these items are actually more profitable for the company .

2) The top and the least profitable products can be identified and accordingly, the promotional strategies can be designed to escalate a product's sales , or to discontinue a product altogether , as per the requirement.

3) Individual states can be studied in terms of sales of each product done in those states , so as to adjust the manufacturing in those areas accordingly.
